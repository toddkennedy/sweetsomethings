SweetSomething's family candy recipes have been a tradition in our family for over a hundred years and now we have decided to open our doors to the public. We hope the public will enjoy our delicious candy as much as our family has for these many many years.

The ingredients we use in our sugar products is nothing but all natural, from the choco in the chocolate, to the sugar in our sugar candies. Our candies are all mouth watering and yummy in the belly, so come on down and try our wide variety of chocolates and candies to choose from.

For our customers who watch their sugar intake and would prefer sugar-free products, we have many delectable sugar-free chocolates and sugar-free candies to choose from.
